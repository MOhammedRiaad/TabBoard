Quick Start

  1. Click the TabBoard icon in your browser toolbar (or press Ctrl+Shift+T)
  2. Create your first board and add folders to organize your tabs
  3. Add tasks in the Today view to start tracking your work
  4. Use the Pomodoro timer to stay focused and track your sessions
